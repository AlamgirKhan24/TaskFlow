Tasks

Click the New Task button in the toolbar on the Tasks page. Fill in the name, project, priority, and due date, then press Save. The task appears immediately in the To Do group.

Click the circle checkbox on the left side of any task row. It turns mint green and the task moves to the Done group. Click it again to restore the task to To Do.

Yes — click directly on the task name text to enter edit mode. Type your changes and then click anywhere else (blur) to save automatically.

Use the status tabs (All / To Do / In Progress / Done) and priority chips (High / Medium / Low) in the filter bar below the toolbar. You can combine them with the search box for precise results.

Kanban Board

Click and hold any card, then drag it to a different column. Release to drop. The card's status updates immediately and syncs to the Tasks page — they share the same data.

To Do — tasks not yet started. In Progress — tasks you are actively working on. Done — completed tasks. Moving a card between columns changes its status in both the Kanban and Tasks views.

Settings

Click the sun / moon icon in the top-right corner of any page, or go to Settings → Appearance and pick a theme there. Your choice is saved and remembered on your next visit.

Go to Settings → Profile. Edit your full name and email, then click Save changes. The sidebar avatar and name update instantly.

Data & Privacy

All TaskFlow data is stored in your browser's localStorage under the flowline: prefix. Nothing is sent to any server — it is entirely local to your device and browser.

Go to Settings → Danger Zone and click Clear data. Confirm in the dialog. This permanently removes all tasks, preferences, and profile data from your browser. It cannot be undone.
